Biography

Hands-on experience in relocation and business on Cyprus

Artur Mardanyan founded ResidentPravo after going through the relocation process personally — facing the same questions clients ask today: how to register a company, open a bank account, obtain residency, and bring a family to Cyprus. That firsthand experience of living and running a business in Cyprus provides a grounded understanding of the challenges entrepreneurs, investors and families face when relocating.

ResidentPravo works with entrepreneurs, investors, professionals and families from different countries who are planning to relocate or grow their business in Cyprus.

ResidentPravo works directly with licensed Cyprus Bar Association lawyers, certified auditors and tax advisors — no intermediaries, no hidden commissions. The team accompanies every client from the initial consultation through to receipt of documents and tax registration.

Consultations are available in Russian, English, Armenian, Ukrainian and Greek.

Key milestones

2014

Relocation to Cyprus

Moving to Cyprus as a permanent resident. Beginning of firsthand study of local legislation, government procedures and the practicalities of living and doing business on the island.

2015

Personal experience navigating the paperwork

Going through the Pink Slip application, bank account opening and settling-in process with the help of licensed lawyers. That direct experience later became the foundation of ResidentPravo's practical guides.

2016

Launch of a Consulting Company

Founded the first consulting company in Cyprus, focusing on business registration, relocation support, and assistance for entrepreneurs. Working directly with clients provided valuable practical experience in immigration procedures, government processes, and the real challenges people face when relocating to Cyprus.

2018

YouTube channel launch & first consultations

Creating a YouTube channel about life and relocation in Cyprus. Beginning to help people planning their move. Over the following years, more than 800 consultations on living, business and immigration in Cyprus.

2022

Founding of ResidentPravo

Launch of ResidentPravo as a platform offering end-to-end support for entrepreneurs, families and professionals on company registration, residency, PR, banking, accounting and settling into life in Cyprus.

2025–2026

ResidentPravo continues to grow

Expanding the library of practical guides, launching expert content and building out detailed resources covering visas, residency, PR, company formation, tax, banking and everyday life in Cyprus.
