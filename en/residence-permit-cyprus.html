Types of Permits

Types of Residence Permits in Cyprus

Cyprus offers several grounds for residency. The right option depends on your situation, budget and goals.

Pink Slip (Temporary Permit)

Temporary residence permit for third-country nationals. Issued for up to 1 year with renewal options. Suitable for those who have just relocated to Cyprus.

from 2 weeks

Residence via Company Registration

The owner or director of a Cyprus company can obtain a residence permit based on business activity. One of the most popular routes for entrepreneurs.

from 2 months

Permanent Residency via Investment (Cat. F)

Permanent residency in Cyprus through an investment of at least €300,000 in qualifying assets. Issued indefinitely — no annual renewal required.

from €300,000

Family Relocation

Family members of a resident (spouse, children under 18, dependent parents) can obtain a permit through family reunification. Processed together with the main applicant.

whole family

Permanent Residency

Permanent Residency in Cyprus via Investment

The Category F programme allows you to obtain indefinite residency in Cyprus with an investment of at least €300,000 in qualifying assets.

The permit is issued permanently and does not require annual renewal. After 5 years you can apply for citizenship.

  • Investment from €300,000 in real estate or securities
  • Confirmed annual income from abroad
  • Clean security background check
  • Extends to spouse and dependent children
  • No obligation to reside in Cyprus permanently
  • Path to citizenship after 5 years

Documents

What you need to apply

The document list depends on the chosen basis. We help with preparation, translation, apostilles and submission.

Core Package (all routes)

  • Valid passport (at least 2 years remaining)
  • Colour biometric photos
  • Medical insurance for the whole family
  • Criminal record certificate (apostilled)
  • Family member documents (where applicable)
  • Official application forms

For Residence via Company

  • Cyprus company incorporation documents
  • Tax Identification Certificate (TIC) and VAT
  • Office lease agreement in Cyprus
  • Company bank account statement
  • Employment contract for director
  • Evidence of business activity

For Permanent Residency (Cat. F)

  • Purchase agreement / investment documents
  • Proof of payment from €300,000
  • Income certificate for last 3 years
  • Bank statement from international bank
  • Source of funds documentation
  • Marriage / birth certificates

For Pink Slip

  • Rental agreement for accommodation in Cyprus
  • Proof of financial means
  • Bank statement for last 3–6 months
  • Proof of income source

Frequently asked questions
